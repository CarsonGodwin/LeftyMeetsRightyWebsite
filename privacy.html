Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

Lefty Meets Righty values your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Info: Name, email address, password, location, and profile photo.
  • Listing and Exchange Data: Photos, descriptions, and messages related to listings and exchanges.
  • Device Data: IP address, operating system, app version, device type.
  • Usage Data: App feature interactions, session duration, and navigation behavior.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Operate and maintain the app.
  • Match users and facilitate exchanges.
  • Improve user experience.
  • Enforce Terms of Service and detect abuse.
  • Communicate with you (e.g., updates or support responses).

We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. Sharing of Information

We share data only:

  • With your consent (e.g., sharing contact info to arrange a meetup).
  • With service providers who assist us (e.g., Firebase, analytics tools).
  • As required by law or to protect user safety or legal rights.

4. Data Retention

We retain user data as long as your account is active or necessary for operations, and in accordance with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data.
  • Request corrections or deletion.
  • Opt out of certain uses.
  • Withdraw consent.

Email us at support@leftymeetsrighty.com to make a request.

6. Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and access controls to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

7. Children’s Privacy

Lefty Meets Righty is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International Users

Your data may be stored or processed outside your country, including in the United States. By using the app, you consent to such data transfers.

9. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy. We’ll notify users of significant changes via in-app alert or email.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related concerns, contact:
support@leftymeetsrighty.com
